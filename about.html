About Us - NONEE

Welcome to NONEE

Welcome to NONEE, the innovative platform designed to revolutionize your fitness journey by providing you with the convenience of an online trainer. Our cutting-edge app scans your entire body to deliver personalized and detailed workout instructions, ensuring you perform exercises with the correct form and get the most out of your fitness regimen.

Description

At NONEE, we understand that starting a workout routine can be challenging and intimidating, especially in a gym environment. Our mission is to make working out accessible and affordable for everyone, regardless of their location or background. With our app, you can achieve your fitness goals comfortably and confidently from the privacy of your home or any other location of your choice.

Milestones

While we are just beginning our journey, we are proud to announce that we have won a grant of 5000 Lari from GITA, which will help us continue developing and improving our services.

Values

  • Accessibility: We believe that fitness should be available to everyone, no matter where they are or who they are.
  • Affordability: Our goal is to provide cost-effective fitness solutions to make a healthy lifestyle within reach for all.
  • Comfort: We aim to help people start and maintain their fitness journey in a comfortable and supportive environment, reducing the intimidation often felt in traditional gym settings.
